Step-by-Step Guide

Navigate your counseling

Clear, step-by-step guidance for JoSAA counseling (IIT/NIT/IIIT) and AP/TS EAPCET admissions.

JoSAA Counseling

For IITs, NITs, IIITs & GFTIs via JEE

1

Register on JoSAA Portal

Visit josaa.nic.in and register using your JEE Main/Advanced roll number. Required for both JEE Main and JEE Advanced qualifiers.

📅 Registration opens 2 days after JEE Advanced results
2

Fill Choice Locking

Add up to 25,000 institute-branch combinations in order of preference. You can modify choices until the lock deadline.

💡 Add 15–20 realistic choices across dream, match & safe colleges
3

Seat Allotment Rounds (6 rounds)

JoSAA conducts 6 rounds. After each round: Accept seat (pays seat acceptance fee) or Float/Slide/Freeze as per preference.

⚡ Float = willing to go higher; Freeze = keep current seat
4

Document Verification & Reporting

Report to your allotted institute with original marksheets, category certificates, medical fitness certificate, and JEE scorecards.

5

CSAB Special Rounds (if needed)

If you miss JoSAA allotment or want to try for vacant seats, CSAB conducts 2 additional special rounds.

🎯 Only for NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs — not IITs

AP/TS EAPCET Counseling

State engineering admissions for Andhra Pradesh & Telangana

1

Certificate Verification (Phase 1)

Visit your allotted helpline centre with originals: 10th, Inter marksheets, Aadhaar, caste certificate, income certificate, and EAPCET hall ticket.

📋 Bring 3 sets of photocopies — verified documents are mandatory
2

Web Options Entry

Fill college–branch preferences online at eapcet-sche.aptonline.in (AP) or tseamcet.nic.in (TS). You can enter up to 100 choices.

💡 Sort by both college quality AND branch — CSE at a B-tier > MECH at an A-tier for most
3

Allotment (3 Phases)

Seats are allotted in 3 phases based on rank, reservations, and web options. Check allotment result and pay tuition fee online to confirm.

4

Self-Reporting at College

After fee payment, download allotment order and report at the college. Carry originals + photocopies. College verifies and issues joining letter.

⏰ Report within 3 days of allotment or seat is cancelled
5

Spot Admissions & Upgrades

Remaining vacant seats are filled via spot admissions. You may also apply for upgrades in subsequent phases if a better option opens.
